Corona Virus News:-

Today 43,263 corona cases and 338 deaths have been registered in India.Today 1,361 covid cases. 15 people died from covid in last 24 hours in Andhra Pradesh.India administered over 715 million vaccine doses till now.India took 85 days to touch 10 crore vaccinations, 45 days to cross the 20-crore mark and 29 more days to reach 30 crore. The country took 24 days to reach 40 crore from 30 crore doses and then 20 more days to cross 50 crore vaccinations on August 6. It took 19 more days to go past the 60-crore mark and took only 13 days to reach 70 crore doses.Kerala to withdraw night curfew, lockdown on Sundays.Chinese researchers found that people infected with Delta carry 1,260 times more in viruses in their noses compared to original coronavirus.

Andhra Pradesh:-

"96.86% of people aged above 45 are vaccinated with 1st dose of covid vaccine. 38.33% of citizens aged between 18-45 have been administered with their first dose.To tackle any adverse event during the possible third wave of Covid-19, as many as 220,964 oxygen concentrators and 27,311 D-type oxygen cylinders have been made available at hospitals by the state government and oxygen pipeline works have successfully been carried out in 108 hospitals.By October 6, 140 hospitals (with over 50 beds each) will be equipped with oxygen generation plants set up by the state government" said CM Jagan Mohan Reddy.
